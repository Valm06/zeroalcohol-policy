Privacy Policy — ZeroAlcohol

Last updated: January 2025

ZeroAlcohol is committed to protecting your privacy. This app provides breastfeeding parents with alcohol-clearance timing tools. We do not collect, store, or share personal data.

Information We Do Not Collect

ZeroAlcohol does not collect or share:

  • Personal information (name, email, address)
  • Account or login data
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Health data stored externally
  • Analytics or usage tracking data

Data Usage

All information you enter (weight, height, drink volume, alcohol percentage) stays locally on your device and is never transmitted to a server.

Purchases

ZeroAlcohol uses the Apple App Store and Google Play billing systems. We do not receive or store payment information. All transactions are handled securely by Apple and Google.

Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of updates.
